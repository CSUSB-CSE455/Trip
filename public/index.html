Whats Trip?

Trip mobile application allows users to notify others of their progress while traveling in terms of time, distance, and location. Users will be able to subscriber to others’ trips and keep track of multiple subscriptions. The idea behind this application is to provide individuals with an easy way to advise others, such as family members or friends, of one’s trip progress towards their destination. Once a user reaches a marker destination, subscribers will receive a notification. When a user creates a trip, estimated time is stored. If that user is being late, others will be notified with certain information. Thus, this application aims to give a peace of mind to those who may be worried about a user being late. To protect users’ privacy, users will be able to choose what information should be shared with others in notifications, such as time, distance, and location. Users will be able to fine-tune those options for each subscriber.

Control

User shall have an ability to specify which information is shared with subscribers: specific location, distance left, time left, or simply that they’ve reached a marker or are being late to a maker without any further details.

Peace of Mind

If a user is being late to a maker, based on time calculated during route planning, Trip will automatically notify subscribers, if the user allows it.

Notifications

Trip will keep a log of all subscription notifications, so that users have access to notifications history.
